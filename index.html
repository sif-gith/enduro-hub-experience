About Us

Enduro Hub Experience is based on over 250 acres of wonderful Cheshire countryside. Our experience days include the bike, all the necessary riding kit and training/guidance with riding where needed. These days are perfect for either complete newbies, experienced riders or anyone in between. Whilst out riding you will come across woodlands, stream crossings, hills, bridges, tunnels, flowing fields and more! We also offer own bike days were you can bring your own Enduro bike and ride for the day with one of our guides. Throughout the day you will get the chance to ride all the different section's/obstacle's. If you want to stay riding one particular section or revisit a section then that's fine and we can do so.

We’re passionate about preserving the environment. Electric bikes deliver high power with minimal noise and zero emissions, allowing us to operate in harmony with nature. Join us to experience sustainable adventure like never before.
