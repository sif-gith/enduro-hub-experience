About Us

Based in Alsager, Cheshire, the Enduro Hub Experience is set across over 250 acres of countryside. Our mission is to introduce riders of all levels to the exhilaration of off‑road riding while embracing innovative electric technology. Whether you choose the ultra‑quiet, zero‑emission Stark Varg or the trusty Honda CRF230F, you’ll receive professional guidance and top‑quality gear.

We’re passionate about preserving the environment. Electric bikes deliver high power with minimal noise and zero emissions, allowing us to operate in harmony with nature. Join us to experience sustainable adventure like never before.
